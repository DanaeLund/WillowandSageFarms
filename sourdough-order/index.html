Order Your Sourdough Bread

  • Limited Weekly Availability

    Since I bake from a home kitchen alongside being a full-time student, I offer a limited number of slots each week.

  • How Pre-Orders Work

    To secure your order for the upcoming week:

    1. Submit your order using the form below by Friday night at 10:00 PM PST.
    2. Orders are fulfilled on a first-come, first-served basis, based on available slots.
    3. Once your order is confirmed, you’ll receive a confirmation email with pickup details.

  • Waitlist Option

    If all slots for the week are full, you can join the waitlist by completing the same form.

    When a slot becomes available:
    You’ll be notified via email or text.
    You’ll have 24 hours to confirm your spot before it’s offered to the next person on the list.

  • Pickup Information

    Pickups are at 8:00 PM PST in Langley, BC, Canada.

  • Payment Methods

    We accept cash and e-transfer.
