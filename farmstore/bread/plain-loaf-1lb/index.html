Classic Loaf

Simple, wholesome, and full of natural flavor. Our Classic Sourdough is made with a mix of bread flour, all-purpose flour, and a touch of whole wheat for depth. Every loaf is hand-shaped and naturally leavened with our sourdough starter, giving it a tender crumb, crisp crust, and that gentle tang that makes sourdough so comforting. Perfect for sandwiches, toast, or just enjoying fresh from the oven.

Ingredients: Unbleached bread flour, all-purpose flour, whole wheat bread flour, water, sourdough starter (flour, water), sea salt.

Prepared with care in our home kitchen, which may also handle common food allergens. Our products are crafted fresh by hand to taste amazing and are not inspected by Fraser Health Authority.
