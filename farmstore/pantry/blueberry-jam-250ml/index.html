Blueberry Jam

Made in small batches with BC blueberries picked at their sweetest. We stir in just a touch of real vanilla to make the berries shine. It is simple and cozy and tastes like summer mornings on toast or swirled into yogurt. We cook every jar right here at Willow and Sage Farms where our family bakes sourdough and grows good food with care for the land. This jam is made using safe home canning methods and comes ready for your breakfasts, baking and late night spoonfuls straight from the jar.

Ingredients: Blueberries, sugar, fruit pectin, vanilla extract, lemon juice.

Prepared with care in our home kitchen, which may also handle common food allergens. Our products are crafted fresh by hand to taste amazing and are not inspected by Fraser Health Authority.
